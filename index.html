Anime refers to a style of animation that originated in Japan and has gained global popularity. Known for its diverse genres, vibrant visuals, and intricate storytelling, anime encompasses everything from action and romance to fantasy and science fiction. It has a dedicated fanbase and continues to influence modern pop culture.

Anime's artistic range is vast, ranging from traditional hand-drawn to computer-generated animation. Its characters often possess exaggerated features, large expressive eyes, and unique hairstyles. The medium's storytelling depth and emotional resonance distinguish it; Studio Ghibli's enchanting tales and "Naruto's" epic adventures showcase its versatility. Anime's impact on entertainment worldwide remains profound and enduring.
